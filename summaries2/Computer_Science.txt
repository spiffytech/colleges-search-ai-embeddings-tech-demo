The Department of Computer Science at NC State University stands at the forefront of technological innovation, dedicated to advancing the field through groundbreaking research and innovative teaching. Our faculty and students collaborate to develop cutting-edge solutions in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and more, fostering a vibrant environment where ideas become transformative technologies. We are committed to producing impactful research that addresses real-world challenges, positioning NC State as a leader in shaping the future of computing.

At the core of our mission is the belief that computing can revolutionize industries, improve lives, and drive economic growth. We emphasize hands-on learning, partnering with industry leaders and community organizations to provide students with practical experience and connections that accelerate their careers. Our programs are designed to nurture diverse talent, promote inclusivity, and prepare graduates who are not only technically proficient but also ethical innovators ready to lead in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

What truly sets us apart is our focus on impactful, applied research that directly benefits society. From securing information systems to developing AI tools for healthcare, our work translates into tangible benefits for communities and industries worldwide. Supported by the latest facilities and a collaborative campus environment, we empower our students and faculty to push the boundaries of what’s possible, ensuring NC State remains a catalyst for technological progress and a trusted partner in solving tomorrow’s hardest problems.
