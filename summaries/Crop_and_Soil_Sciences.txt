Crop and Soil Sciences addresses global challenges of food security and sustainability through innovative research. Our digital tools like the Cover Crop Selector help farmers make data-driven decisions for sustainable agriculture.

Undergraduate programs offer hands-on learning with customizable electives in agronomy, biotechnology, and environmental science. Students gain field experience at research stations across North Carolina.

Extension programs bridge research and practice through training and publications. The Plant Disease and Insect Clinic provides diagnostic services supporting agricultural productivity statewide.

As part of NC State's College of Agriculture and Life Sciences, we advance sustainable solutions through research and extension that strengthen North Carolina's agricultural economy.
