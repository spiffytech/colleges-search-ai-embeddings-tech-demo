Philosophy and Religious Studies develops critical thinkers through rigorous examination of fundamental questions. Our philosophy program explores knowledge, reality and ethics while religious studies analyzes religion's societal impact.

Specialized minors in Cognitive Science, Ethics, and Health/Medicine bridge disciplines. The department leads in applied ethics research, including groundbreaking work on AI morality for driverless cars.

Honors programs and undergraduate research opportunities foster deep scholarship. Faculty collaborate across colleges to address complex societal challenges through humanistic inquiry.

As part of NC State's College of Humanities and Social Sciences, we cultivate engaged citizens who apply critical thinking to address contemporary ethical and social challenges.
