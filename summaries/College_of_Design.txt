As a premier design institution, we champion human-centered innovation that transforms industries and communities. Our rigorous programs in architecture, industrial design, and emerging media arts prepare students to solve complex problems through creative thinking and technical excellence.

We differentiate through unique facilities like the Virtual Production Lab - one of the most advanced academic VR environments in the Southeast - and industry partnerships that provide real-world experience. Our Design Camp for K-12 education inspires future designers while generating community impact.

Signature initiatives include the Health Centered Design Lab improving patient experiences and the Pappas Real Estate Development Program creating sustainable urban solutions. We empower designers to shape a more equitable and beautiful world.
