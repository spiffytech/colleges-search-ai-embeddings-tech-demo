The School of Architecture prepares students to solve contemporary urban and environmental challenges through accredited B.Arch and M.Arch programs. Our integrated Path to Architectural Licensure (iPAL) accelerates professional development while maintaining rigorous design standards.

Unique research initiatives like the Building Energy Technology Lab and Public Interest Design Incubator address sustainable building solutions. Industry partnerships provide real-world experience through community-driven Design+Build projects and sponsored studios.

Specialized certificates in Disaster Resilience, City Design, and Energy Technology allow focused expertise development. Global study programs and cross-disciplinary collaborations foster innovative approaches to equitable community development.

As part of NC State's College of Design, we champion human-centered innovation through advanced facilities like the Virtual Production Lab, creating sustainable solutions for more equitable communities.
