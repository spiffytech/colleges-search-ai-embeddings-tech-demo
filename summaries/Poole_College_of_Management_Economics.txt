Economics at Poole College combines rigorous theoretical training with applied policy analysis. Our faculty have national recognition for research in areas like international trade, labor markets, and financial economics.

The Devils and Wolves Seminar Series brings leading economists to campus, fostering intellectual exchange. PhD candidates gain strong placement in academia, government, and private sector research roles.

Undergraduate programs develop analytical skills for careers in finance, policy, and consulting. Students apply economic principles to real-world challenges through case studies and research projects.

As part of NC State's Poole College of Management, we advance economic understanding through research that informs policy and business decision-making in a global economy.
