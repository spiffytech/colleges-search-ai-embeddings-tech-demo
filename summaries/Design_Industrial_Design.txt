Industrial Design at NC State develops creative problem-solvers through NASAD-accredited programs focused on user-centered design. Students study human interaction with artifacts to create innovative products balancing form, function and sustainability.

Industry partnerships with companies like Adidas and Under Armour provide real-world experience through sponsored studios. Our new dedicated design space fosters collaboration while digital fabrication labs enable rapid prototyping.

The Design It Forward initiative emphasizes social impact, preparing graduates to address challenges like period poverty. Alumni lead at companies ranging from medical device firms to consumer brands, driving human-centered innovation.

As part of NC State's College of Design, we leverage industry partnerships and facilities like the Materials Lab to create solutions that transform industries through equitable, sustainable design.
