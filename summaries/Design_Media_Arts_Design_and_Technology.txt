Media Arts, Design and Technology pioneers interdisciplinary creative practice through animation, game design, and interactive media. Our program blends traditional craftsmanship with emerging technologies like VR/AR and digital fabrication.

Research initiatives through the Co-Lab and Experience Design Lab explore new forms of storytelling. Industry-sponsored projects provide professional experience while entrepreneurial opportunities like Art2Wear showcase student innovation.

The Visual Narrative Cluster fosters experimental approaches to digital storytelling. Graduates lead in entertainment, educational technology, and experience design, creating engaging artifacts that impact culture and learning.

As part of NC State's College of Design, we leverage advanced facilities like the Virtual Production Lab to transform industries through human-centered innovation and creative technology.
