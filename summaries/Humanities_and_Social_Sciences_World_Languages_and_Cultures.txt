World Languages and Cultures offers unique programs in over a dozen languages, including Hindi-Urdu, Korean, and Portuguese. Our Project GO initiative provides ROTC students intensive training in critical languages for military service.

The TESOL program features high-impact international teaching experiences, like the Costa Rica partnership. VOLAR community engagement connects Spanish students with local Latino communities.

Accelerated BA/MA pathways allow efficient advanced study. Graduates develop cultural competence for careers in global business, education, and government.

As part of NC State's College of Humanities and Social Sciences, we prepare globally engaged citizens through language mastery and cross-cultural understanding.
