We drive scientific discovery through interdisciplinary research tackling global challenges in health, energy and environment. Our top-ranked mathematical sciences programs and Goldwater Scholarship winners demonstrate excellence in STEM education.

The Integrative Sciences Initiative creates collaborative spaces for breakthrough innovation, while the Data Science and AI Academy prepares students for emerging technologies. Unique fossil research reshapes our understanding of prehistoric ecosystems.

Signature programs include the Science House K-12 outreach inspiring future scientists and climate research through the State Climate Office. We cultivate critical thinkers who transform data into solutions for humanity's greatest challenges.
