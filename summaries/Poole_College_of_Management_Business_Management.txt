Business Management integrates four specialized disciplines: Finance, IT/Business Analytics, Marketing, and Operations/Supply Chain. Our interdisciplinary approach prepares students for complex business challenges through practical, industry-relevant education.

Faculty expertise spans cutting-edge areas like fintech, digital marketing, and sustainable supply chains. Students gain real-world experience through case competitions, corporate projects, and executive education partnerships.

The department fosters innovation through cross-disciplinary collaboration. Graduates lead in diverse sectors including consulting, technology, and global manufacturing.

As part of NC State's Poole College of Management, we develop agile business leaders who drive innovation and create value in a technology-driven economy.
