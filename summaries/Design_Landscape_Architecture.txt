Landscape Architecture and Environmental Planning offers a STEM-designated MLA program focused on resilient community design. Our Coastal Dynamics Lab and Just Communities Lab pioneer solutions for climate adaptation and social equity through evidence-based research.

The Pappas Real Estate Development Program creates sustainable urban solutions while the Natural Learning Initiative designs child-centered environments. Students gain hands-on experience through Design+Build projects addressing real community challenges.

Specialized certificates in City Design and Disaster Resilience prepare graduates for leadership roles in sustainable development. Alumni transform communities through projects that balance ecological health with human wellbeing.

As part of NC State's College of Design, we champion human-centered innovation through industry partnerships and advanced facilities, creating sustainable solutions for more equitable communities.
