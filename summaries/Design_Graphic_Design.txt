The Graphic & Experience Design program uniquely blends UX/UI with traditional graphic design, preparing students for careers at leading tech firms. Our human-centered research methods ensure designs solve real user needs while exploring emerging technologies like VR and AI.

Industry-sponsored studios provide professional experience through real client projects. Students develop portfolios showcasing expertise in branding, service design, and interactive systems that empower users.

Specialized graduate journal "And So" and thesis showcase "Then Finally" highlight research excellence. Alumni lead innovation at companies like IBM, Pentagram, and IDEO, driving user-centered digital transformation.

As part of NC State's College of Design, we leverage advanced facilities like the Virtual Production Lab to create experiences that transform industries and communities through human-centered innovation.
