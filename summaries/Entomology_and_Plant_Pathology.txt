Entomology and Plant Pathology ranks #5 globally for entomology programs, focusing on sustainable pest and disease management. Our Plant Disease and Insect Clinic provides vital diagnostic services supporting agriculture statewide.

Unique facilities like the NC State Insect Collection and Mycological Herbarium serve as national research resources. We study both harmful and beneficial organisms, developing biocontrol solutions for sustainable agriculture.

Extension programs translate research into practical solutions for farmers and communities. Students gain hands-on experience through field projects and industry partnerships.

As part of NC State's College of Agriculture and Life Sciences, we protect crops, ecosystems and human health through innovative research and extension services.
