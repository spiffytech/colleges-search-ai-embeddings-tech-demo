We cultivate critical thinkers who navigate complex human challenges through interdisciplinary study of communication, ethics, and cultural systems. Our programs develop the analytical and communication skills essential for leadership in business, law, and public service.

Unique initiatives include the Faculty Fellows program advancing critical thinking pedagogy and research on AI ethics for autonomous vehicles. Our Humanities Extension Program brings scholarly expertise to community challenges across North Carolina.

Signature events like The Human Factor speaker series feature prominent thinkers exploring human-centered solutions. We prepare graduates to bridge divides and drive meaningful change in an increasingly complex world.
