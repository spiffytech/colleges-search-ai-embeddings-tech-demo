Wilson College of Textiles stands as North America's only dedicated textiles college, offering comprehensive programs from fiber science to fashion design. The college partners with industry leaders like Nike and Under Armour on real-world projects through its Senior Design program. Students gain hands-on experience in world-class facilities including the Nonwovens Institute and Textile Protection Center.

The curriculum integrates sustainability and innovation across all disciplines, preparing students for careers in technical textiles and advanced materials. Unique study abroad programs at institutions like London College of Fashion provide global industry exposure. Corporate partnerships yield exceptional outcomes like 100% internship placement for textile engineering majors.

Wilson College drives textile innovation through research in smart fabrics, sustainable materials, and protective textiles. Industry-sponsored capstones solve pressing challenges like heat-stress workwear and washable electronics. These industry connections create direct pathways to careers at Fortune 500 companies and innovative startups.
